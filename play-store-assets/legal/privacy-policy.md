# Privacy Policy for HowManyHours

**Last updated: [DATE]**

## Information We Collect

HowManyHours is designed to respect your privacy. The app collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

### Data Collected:
- **Project Names**: Names of time tracking projects you create
- **Time Entries**: Start/stop timestamps for tracking sessions
- **Duration Data**: Calculated time spent on projects
- **Project Selection**: Which project is currently active

### Data NOT Collected:
- Personal identifying information
- Location data
- Contact information
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports

## How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally on your Android device using Android's Room database (SQLite). No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

### Storage Details:
- **Location**: Internal app storage (not accessible to other apps)
- **Format**: SQLite database
- **Encryption**: Protected by Android's app sandboxing
- **Backup**: Included in Android's app data backup (if enabled by user)

## Data Usage

Your data is used solely for:
- Displaying your time tracking information
- Calculating project durations and monthly summaries
- Providing timing notifications while tracking is active

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. All data remains on your device.

## Data Control

### Access Your Data:
- All data is visible within the app interface
- No hidden data collection

### Delete Your Data:
- Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data
- Individual projects can be deleted within the app
- No server-side data to delete

### Export Your Data:
- App includes CSV export functionality
- Data can be exported to external storage for backup

## Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
