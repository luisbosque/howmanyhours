- Does your app allow unrestricted web access? **NO**

- Can users share content with others? **NO** (local storage only)

### Miscellaneous
- Does your app contain ads? **NO**
- Does your app use location services? **NO**
- Does your app access sensitive device permissions? **NO** (only basic storage and notifications)

## Expected Rating: 
**Everyone** - Safe for all audiences

## Rationale:
Simple productivity app for time tracking with no social features, advertising, or sensitive content. Users only create project names and track time locally.
